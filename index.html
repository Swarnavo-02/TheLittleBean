Our Story

Since 1995, The Little Bean has been brewing more than just coffee — we've been serving Kolkata a taste of global craftsmanship with a local soul. We handpick beans from the world's most celebrated coffee regions, curating blends that bring out both the richness of origin and the warmth of home.

At the heart of our café are passionate roasters and baristas who honor tradition while embracing creativity. From the misty highlands of Ethiopia to the bustling spirit of Kolkata’s own coffee culture, every cup tells a story — bold, balanced, and brewed with care.
